Abstract

Spatiotemporal Skip Guidance (STG) is a novel training-free guidance method for video diffusion models. By employing layer skipping, STG produces aligned weak models to enhance sample quality without additional training. This approach improves both diversity and temporal consistency in video generation while reducing computational costs.

Methodology

STG leverages layer skipping techniques such as Residual Skip and Attention Skip:

  • Residual Skip: Skips residual blocks to create degraded versions of the input.
  • Attention Skip: Perturbs spatiotemporal attention layers to guide sampling towards higher quality.
